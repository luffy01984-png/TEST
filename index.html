About Youcef IT

Youcef IT is a modern and inspiring school located in the beautiful city of Brighton, UK. Our mission is to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where students can develop both academically and personally. We pride ourselves on our inclusive community, innovative teaching methods, and strong focus on creativity and critical thinking.

School Badge: Our badge features a green circuit tree on a gold background, symbolizing growth, knowledge, and technology. The school colours are green and gold, representing harmony, success, and ambition.
